### MemryX MX3 Chip
 
* Up to 6 TFLOPs (1GHz) per chip with high utilization
* Up to 16-chips (96 TOPS/TFLOPs) can be interconnected

* Batch = 1
* 10.5M 8-bit parameters (weights) per chip
* PCIe Gen 3 I/O or USB 3 interface to host
* 0.6-2W per chip average power (AI model and system config dependent)
